The Story of Me

I suppose the first sparks of interests in Geography and cities environment was formed by my globe atlas stand, which provided me flows of curiosity about the countries, locations and the layers they demonstrate! This has been followed by further studies as Urban Planner in the university to recognize the network of dimensions and complexities interacting with each other for a better human living. As I have experienced different academic and professional environment from Iran to Italy, they were supported by studying Geography, Economy, Social science, Policy development, Urban planning theories, getting familiar with different approaches with international professors.
Here I am at city of Salzburg, studying Erasmus+ programme, Copernicus Master In Digital Earth to improve my knowledge in topics concerning Earth, where the real story begins.
