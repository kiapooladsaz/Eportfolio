A few words

Following the request from Copernicus Master In Digital Earth programme, this website intends to demonstrate collection of professional and personal works and interests regarding remote sensing, satellite imagery analysis, GIS and geospatial analysis and urban planning. The main reason behind this website is exposing my interests based on different particular topics to the readers and also as a self motivation to expand and improve my works. In addition, I believe it could provide a hub to receive feedbacks, recommendations and interests from the readers and all in all, the website would be continuously updated.
